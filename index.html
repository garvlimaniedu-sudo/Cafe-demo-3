Our Story

Made for the pause
between moments.

Fresh Bite began as a quiet idea: that a cup of coffee deserves the same care as anything else you slow down for. Every detail here, the light, the ceramics, the hush between tables, is chosen so you have one fewer reason to rush.

We roast in small batches, pour with intention, and keep the room as calm as the coffee is strong.
